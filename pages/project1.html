Headroom

What is this?

Headroom is for engineers, musicians, and those in the audio industry. Intended as a mediating tool to ease the organizational and transactional facets of studio recording & performing, specifically, Headroom allows for users to create studio listings, book studios, rent equipment, and access studio recordings through file centralization.
